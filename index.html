Areas of Focus

Technology & Human Flourishing

Creating products that genuinely serve people rather than exploit them—through building technology that enriches life's most important domains: physical and mental health, happiness and life satisfaction, meaning and purpose, and social relationships.

AI & Human Potential

Developing AI as collaborative partners that amplify human capability rather than replace it—through creating systems that enhance our thinking, accelerate learning, and support decision-making while preserving human agency and creativity.

Interdisciplinarity & Responsible Innovation

Ensuring innovation anticipates and prevents unintended consequences—through actively integrating cross-disciplinary perspectives from the start while maintaining team focus and efficiency.
